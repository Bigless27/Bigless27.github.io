Lets talk about what prototyping is in JavaScript. At first this may seem like a scary topic, that may be hard to learn. Not to worry. because with this cheat sheet you will become a master on prototyping.

I will start by explaining what is going on in the above example. As you can see I am creating a constructor function called Fish(), which does not except any parameters.
